Welcome to the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research Lab.

The Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research lab (BAIR) has a long history of innovation in all areas of AI, including computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing and robotics. Some of its current main thrusts include deep learning, human-compatible AI / AI for good, and connecting AI with other disciplines, including science and humanities.
